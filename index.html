Be Your Own Bartender

Standardization is usually a good thing, but it can lead to a impasse when it comes to innovation. We want to challenge this industry standard with our own MIXIT™ mechanism: Turn the key to connect the liquor with the soda, pull the handle to start the turbofan, twist open the screw cap and enjoy the ride!

Partake In An Experience

We want to provide you with an experience, just like when you open a can of Coke: *sshhh*. Aah, feels good right? We want you to MIXIT together with your friends and share the mesmerizing experience when the two liquids mix into one. And then enjoy the delicious content of course! Have a lovely journey!

Let's make premixes fun again!

Started With Brown Liquor

Sidevogn Fizz™ is our two ingredient premix line-up, and it all started with the beloved Whiskey & Coke. However, we acknowledge that the rough & tough experience brown liquor brings might be off-putting to some. That's when we decided to make a more fun & playful variation that appeals to a broader audience!

Got White Liquor As Well

What better way of inaugurating our take on white liquor than with the delicous Gin & Tonic? We've reduced the alcohol percentage compared to our stronger brown liquor product variation to allow for a smooth & easy drinking experience. And hey you! More exiting tastes are coming soon. Stay tuned!

PETER & PETER

The men behind the bar

It All Began In A Bar ...

We sat in a bar in Stockholm a cold winter night in 2017, and got enthralled by the beauty of the liquor bottles standing majestically on the shelf. That's when we decided on making a liquor company. Fast forward half a year, we drove around Funen on Puch Maxi scooters in fitting attire. An idea was born.

What About The Sidecar?

Later that year I got to sit in on a motorcyclist, whom of course had a sidecar attached to his motorcycle. And thus, a brand was born. Now we both got our motorcycle license, and if you're lucky, you can see us cruising around the streets of Copenhagen with our delicious Sidevogn Cocktails aboard. *Vrooom!*
